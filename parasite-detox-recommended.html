How to make it

Blend a base bowl or smoothie: yogurt or kefir with blueberries, flaxseed, chia, and pumpkin seeds.
Add fresh ginger to the blend, or sip ginger tea alongside it.
Use garlic and bitter greens in that day's meals rather than in the smoothie.
Drink water across the day so the added fiber moves smoothly.
Keep it food-level for 1–2 weeks, track how you feel, and reassess instead of escalating to strong herbs.

Careful with

Introduce fiber gradually and drink enough water — too much too fast causes bloating or constipation.
Skip anything you react to (seeds, dairy); use a dairy-free fermented option if dairy bothers you.
Garlic and ginger at high or supplement-level doses can worsen reflux or interact with blood thinners.
Blood in stool, fever, severe pain, dehydration, or unexplained weight loss means medical care — not a blend.
