Daily Planner

Work Meal Planner

Plan what you'll actually eat at work today. Set each meal to at work, at home, or skip — the plan focuses on your work meals and adjusts the focus when you eat earlier or skip. Your choices are saved on this device.

How it works. Tick foods as you pack or eat them. If you already ate before work, set Morning to at home; if you'll eat once at work and again at home later, set the others accordingly. The "focus" under each work meal shifts based on what you've eaten so far. General guidance, not medical or specific dietary advice.
