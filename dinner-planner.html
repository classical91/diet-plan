Dinner Planner

Your one real meal at home each night, built to balance. Compose a dinner from a protein, one carb or legume, one or two vegetables, and an optional sauce — then drop it onto any day of the week. Flip a day to ordering out when you don't cook. Everything saves on this device.

The dinner formula. Dinner = protein + 1 carb/legume + 1–2 vegetables + optional healthy fat or sauce. Keep it to a single starchy carb — rice and potato and yam in one plate is too much. Legumes (lentils, beans, chickpeas) bring fibre and plant protein, so they pair well with lighter sides. General guidance, not medical or specific dietary advice.
