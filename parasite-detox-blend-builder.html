Before you blend:

Stacking many strong herbs at once is the most common mistake. Confirm a diagnosis first when symptoms persist, keep strong herbs practitioner-guided, and avoid intense cleanses if pregnant, breastfeeding, immunocompromised, on medication, or managing liver, kidney, seizure, or bleeding conditions.
