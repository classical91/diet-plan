Rich Foods for Key Nutrients

Use these quick tables to pick whole-food sources for potassium, magnesium, zinc, vitamins, electrolytes, and the other nutrients tracked in the nutrition checklist. Values are approximate and vary by brand, preparation, and serving size.

Tip: rotate foods across the week instead of relying on one source every day. Ask a qualified clinician before using supplements for diagnosed deficiencies or medical conditions.
